GERALDINE MORALES

creative front-end developer
based in South Florida

Hello! I’m Gigi. A web developer with a passion for creative design and interaction. I graduated with a BS in Exercise Science and Nutrition from Simmons College in Boston, and since then have shifted my focus to developing creative and seamless mobile-first web experiences.

I was always intrigued with the idea that with web development you can work on any project pertaining to any industry and have the opportunity to meet so many different people with different perspectives. I also love the fact that writing one line of code can start you on a journey of building and creating some really amazing applications that can better the lives of people. This led me to attend General Assembly’s Web Development Immersive Bootcamp Program. During my 10-12 hour days with General Assembly, I had the opportunity to dive deeper into web development and learn more on both frontend and backend development. I’ve now been writing code for ~2 years and am always trying to learn new things and keep growing.

Currently building awesome things @ rgba, a remote creative team focused on building elegant web and app experiences for modern brands. I am also available for freelance work and new opportunities.

If you have a cool project or collaboration in mind, get in touch!
